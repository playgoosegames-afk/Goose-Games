About Penalty Shots

Simple, fun, and addictive penalty kick game! Pull back and shoot to score goals. Trending at #8 on Scratch — easy to pick up but hard to master. How many can you score?

How to Play

Click and drag backward to aim your shot, then release to kick. Pick your spot!

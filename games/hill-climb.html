About Hill Climb Racing

Based on the hugely popular mobile game! Drive your car over hills and valleys, collecting coins and reaching the finish line without flipping. Physics-based fun for all ages.

How to Play

Right arrow to accelerate, left arrow to brake/reverse. Balance your speed to avoid flipping!

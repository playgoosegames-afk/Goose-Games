About Blockhead

A Game Jam quality puzzle platformer with a polished story mode. Blockhead is one of the most impressive Scratch games ever made — move blocks strategically to solve each level and uncover the story.

How to Play

Arrow keys to move. Use blocks to solve puzzles and reach the exit.

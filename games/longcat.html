LongCat

Puzzle

About LongCat

A satisfying fill-the-space puzzle game inspired by Fancade. Guide your cat to fill every tile on the grid. Simple to learn, surprisingly deep — perfect for puzzle lovers!

How to Play

Arrow keys or swipe to move. Fill every cell to complete the level!

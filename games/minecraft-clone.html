About Minecraft Clone

A full Minecraft-style sandbox in your browser! Explore multiple dimensions, build with 100+ block types, and save your world to continue later. One of the most impressive browser games you'll find.

How to Play

WASD to move, mouse to look. Left click to break blocks, right click to place. E to open inventory.

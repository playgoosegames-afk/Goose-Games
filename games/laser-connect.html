About Laser Connect

Laser Connect is a unique cooperative puzzle game that requires two players working together to succeed! Guide lasers through mirrors, switches, and obstacles to hit all the targets. Communication and coordination are key as each player controls different elements of the puzzle.

Perfect for siblings, friends, or parent-child teams! This game teaches valuable collaboration skills while challenging your spatial reasoning and problem-solving abilities.

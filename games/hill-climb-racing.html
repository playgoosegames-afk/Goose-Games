About Hill Climb Racing

Experience the thrill of physics-based hill climbing! Navigate challenging terrain, perform flips, and see how far you can go in this addictive racing game based on the popular mobile game.

Skills developed:

  • ⚖️ Balance & Control - Master vehicle physics
  • 🎯 Timing - Perfect your acceleration
  • 🧠 Physics Understanding - Learn about momentum and gravity

Created by: @WO997 on Scratch

How to Play

🎮 Controls

  • Up Arrow - Accelerate
  • Down Arrow - Brake
  • Left/Right - Balance vehicle

💡 Tips

  • Balance is key - don't flip over!
  • Use momentum on downhills
  • Land on your wheels

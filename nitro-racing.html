About Nitro Racing

The ultimate racing game on Goose Games! Nitro Racing features a full story mode, online multiplayer, a track editor, and 18 missions to complete. Race against friends or strangers online — this is one of the most feature-rich games on the platform.

How to Play

Arrow keys or WASD to drive. Follow the in-game tutorial for multiplayer and track editor.

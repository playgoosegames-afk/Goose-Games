About LongCat

LongCat is an adorable fill-the-space puzzle game inspired by the popular mobile game Fancade. Stretch your cat to fill every space on the grid without leaving any gaps. It's simple to learn but challenging to master!

This charming puzzle game helps develop spatial reasoning and planning skills in a fun, accessible way.

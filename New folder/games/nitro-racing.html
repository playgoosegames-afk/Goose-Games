About Nitro Racing

Get ready for the ultimate racing experience! Nitro Racing is an elite multiplayer racing game that combines fast-paced action with strategic gameplay. Race through 18 exciting missions, challenge friends in online multiplayer, or create your own custom tracks with the built-in track editor!

This game teaches important skills like:

  • 🎯 Timing & Coordination - Master the perfect moments to use your nitro boost
  • 🧠 Strategic Thinking - Choose the best racing lines and when to take risks
  • 🎨 Creativity - Design and build your own custom race tracks
  • 🤝 Sportsmanship - Compete fairly in multiplayer races

Created by: @alexandretherrien on Scratch

How to Play

🎮 Controls

  • Arrow Keys - Steer your car left and right, accelerate and brake
  • SPACE - Activate nitro boost (use wisely!)
  • ESC - Pause game / Open menu

🏁 Game Modes

  • Story Mode - Complete 18 exciting missions to unlock new cars and tracks
  • Multiplayer - Race against friends online in real-time
  • Track Editor - Build your own custom race tracks and share them
  • Time Trial - Race against the clock for the best times

💡 Pro Tips

  • Save your nitro boost for straightaways to get maximum speed!
  • Take corners wide for better control and faster exit speeds
  • Watch the minimap to see where other racers are
  • Experiment with different cars - each has unique handling!

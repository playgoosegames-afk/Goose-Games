About Goose Games 🦆

Free browser games for kids — no downloads, no fuss.

What is Goose Games?

Goose Games is a free, curated gaming platform for kids ages 6–16. We hand-pick the best browser games across puzzle, racing, sports, and creative categories — all playable instantly with no downloads required.

Meet Gus the Goose

Gus is our friendly mascot who loves discovering great games! He's always on the lookout for the next awesome title to add to the collection.

Our Mission

We believe great games should be accessible to everyone. Every game on Goose Games is free to play, kid-appropriate, and works right in your browser. No accounts needed, no downloads, just games!

Game Sources

Our games come from talented creators on Scratch and indie developers on Itch.io. All games are used with proper attribution or explicit developer permission. Want to see your game on Goose Games? Submit it here!

Laser Connect

🧩 Puzzle 2 Player

About Laser Connect

A unique 2-player cooperative puzzle game! Work together with a friend to connect lasers, redirect beams, and solve increasingly complex levels. Communication is key!

How to Play

Player 1: Arrow keys. Player 2: WASD. Both players must cooperate to solve each puzzle.
